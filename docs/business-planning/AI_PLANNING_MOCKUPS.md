These mockups demonstrate how the AI-supported conversational business planning interface would look and feel in real-world usage scenarios. The design emphasizes:

1. **Natural conversation flow** with AI facilitating discussions
2. **Real-time simulation integration** showing immediate impact of decisions
3. **Professional interface** suitable for executive meetings
4. **Clear consensus tracking** and decision validation
5. **Mobile-optimized views** for meeting room tablets
6. **Comprehensive documentation** with automated summaries and action items

The interface transforms complex spreadsheet-based planning into collaborative conversations while maintaining the analytical rigor that business planning requires.
